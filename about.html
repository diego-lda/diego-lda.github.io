About Me

I am a Economics PhD candidate at the University of Bristol in the UK, my expectation is to submit my thesis in October 2022. My research looks at the relationship between cryptocurrency prices and the network structure of individual transactions that take place utilising the blockchain protocols they are built on. In particular, focusing on Bitcoin.

Alongside my postgraduate studies I have been involved in teaching assistance at the masters level and research assistance for an academic paper. While doing this I contributed to the creation, documentation and maintenance of an R package.

Between my undergraduate and postgraduate studies I worked in the front office of BNP Paribas Wealth Management in Spain. In this role I developed a website to manage advisory contracts as well as a new management tool for the structured products clients held.

In my free time, I am a dedicated water polo player, competing at the national level as well as being involved in coaching junior players.
