About Me

My formal training is in economics, econometrics and finance. I also have working experience in the banking sector and have dedicated time to software development.

COMPLEX DATA

ECONOMETRICS

COMMUNICATION SKILLS

Delivering key data science in a variety of landscapes whilst ensuring team efficiency.
